The Goal-Oriented Academy

(GOA) is an educational initiative that focuses on

equipping students with both technical and personal development skills. It offers courses

in programming, technology, and modern business practices, while also emphasizing the

importance of soft skills like communication and teamwork

The academy provides a variety of resources, including video tutorials and masterclasses,

to help learners achieve their goals. For example, they cover topics like Python, JavaScript

game development, and even robotics. Additionally, GOA organizes hackathons and

interactive sessions to foster creativity and innovation

The Goal-Oriented Academy (GOA) offers a variety of courses designed to prepare students for modern business and technology challenges. Here's an overview of their offerings:

Technology & Development:

Web Development

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science

Game Development

Creative & Filmmaking:

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe Illustrator

DaVinci Resolve

Entrepreneurship:

Business Model Creation and Planning

Marketing and Sales

Financial Management

Team Building

Pitching and Fundraising

Visual Branding and Graphic Design

Programming:

Python (with a focus on preparing for tech interviews)

JavaScript

HTML and CSS

Robotics and Electrical Engineering:

Hands-on projects and lessons in robotics and engineering concepts.

GOA also emphasizes soft skills like creative thinking, leadership, and social networking. Their educational approach is built on the "4C" principle: Connect, Construct, Contemplate, and Continue, ensuring a well-rounded learning experience.
